About

Highly experienced Consultant Anaesthetist committed to delivering high-quality, patient-centred perioperative care. Specialising in Neuroanaesthesia and the implementation of personalised anaesthesia protocols to optimise patient outcomes. Dedicated to enhancing surgical recovery through perioperative prehabilitation and leveraging expertise in healthcare consulting and medical devices to drive sector-wide improvements.

Specialist interests

Neuroanaesthesia

Anaesthetic care for neurosurgical and neuro-interventional procedures, with a focus on safety, physiology, and perioperative outcomes.

Spinal surgery

Perioperative anaesthetic management for complex spine surgery, supporting enhanced recovery and optimised outcomes.

Interventional neuroradiology

Anaesthesia for neuro-interventional radiology procedures, supporting stable conditions and rapid recovery.

Endovascular stroke treatment

Anaesthetic support for endovascular treatment of stroke, emphasising timely workflow and optimal neurological outcomes.

Orthopaedic surgery

Anaesthesia for orthopaedic surgery (including arthroplasty), with attention to analgesia, mobility and rapid recovery.

Cosmetic surgery

Perioperative anaesthetic management for cosmetic surgical procedures, supporting optimal patient experience and satisfaction.
